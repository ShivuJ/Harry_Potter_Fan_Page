Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, the famous school from the Harry Potter series, has several logos and crests associated with it. The most recognizable crest features a shield divided into four sections, with each section representing one of the four Hogwarts houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin.

But since Harry never submitted his name for the Tournament, who did?
